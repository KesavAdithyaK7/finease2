About Systematic Savings

A Systematic Savings Plan helps you build a disciplined saving habit by setting aside a fixed amount regularly. This approach helps you reach your financial goals while earning interest on your savings.

Benefits:

  • Regular savings discipline
  • Compound interest growth
  • Goal-based saving approach
  • Flexible and customizable

Savings Tips

50-30-20 Rule

  • 50% for needs
  • 30% for wants
  • 20% for savings

Smart Saving Strategies

  • Automate your savings
  • Save windfalls and bonuses
  • Track your progress regularly
