What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is a method of investing in mutual funds where you invest a fixed amount regularly instead of making a lump sum investment.

Key Benefits:

  • Regular, disciplined investing
  • Rupee cost averaging
  • Power of compounding
  • Lower risk through systematic approach
  • Suitable for beginners

Investment Rules

5-3-2 Rule

  • 50% for essential expenses
  • 30% for non-essential expenses
  • 20% for savings & investments

8-4-3 Rule

  • 8% annual return expectation
  • 4X growth potential in 15 years
  • 3X growth potential in 10 years
